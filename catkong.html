🐱 Cat Kong

Time: 60
Dreamies: 0
Lives Lost: 0
Climb the ladders and reach HOME at the top-left of the tower. Collect Dreamies for extra points, but beware of the dogs roaming the platforms. Your final score is Dreamies collected plus time remaining.
Swipe on mobile or use the arrow keys on desktop. Dogs will usually climb down ladders, but not always. Decide whether to take the risky route for more Dreamies or race straight home.

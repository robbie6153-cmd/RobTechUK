About RobTechUK

RobTechUK is an independent games project, currently focusing on building simple, brain training online games.

The company was founded in March 2026 in Manchester, when i wanted to create a games app based on a workplace game we used to play, but was put off by the £20-30,000 price quoted by professional developers.

I started to think, maybe I can create these games myself, and decided to learn games logic, creating simple games in the process.

After creating Letters In as the first project, new word game ideas started to follow and RobTechUK was born. RobTechUK fully intend to be expanding into new ideas very soon.

We are constantly updating our games to give you the best possible experience. If you have any questions or suggestions, contact us at robtechuk@hotmail.com.

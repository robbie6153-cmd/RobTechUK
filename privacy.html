Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

RobTechUK is an independent UK tech and games project. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use RobTechUK websites, games, accounts, leaderboards, and related features.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Account information such as your email address and username
  • Gameplay data such as scores, rankings, progress, game statistics, and leaderboard entries
  • Poll or voting activity where relevant to RobTechUK projects
  • Basic technical data such as browser type, device type, IP address, and pages visited
  • Analytics data to help us understand how our websites and games are used

2. Accounts and Login

If you create a RobTechUK account, your account may be used across multiple RobTechUK games and services. Account features may include login, usernames, profiles, leaderboards, saved scores, game statistics, and other account-based features.

Account data may be stored using third-party services such as Firebase Authentication and Firebase Firestore. These services help us securely manage accounts, login, and game data.

3. Leaderboards and Public Data

If you submit a score to a leaderboard, your username, score, ranking, and related game information may be visible to other users. Please do not use a username that contains personal information you do not want to be public.

4. Cookies and Local Storage

RobTechUK may use cookies, localStorage, or similar browser storage to:

  • Save game progress and preferences
  • Keep you signed in
  • Store local scores or settings
  • Improve site performance and user experience

5. Analytics and Advertising

We may use services such as Google Analytics to understand how users interact with our websites. We may also use Google AdSense or similar advertising services to display adverts.

These services may use cookies or similar technologies to measure traffic, prevent fraud, personalise or limit advertising, and improve ad performance.

6. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Create and manage user accounts
  • Run games, leaderboards, profiles, and account features
  • Store scores, rankings, and gameplay statistics
  • Improve RobTechUK websites, games, and services
  • Fix bugs, improve security, and prevent abuse
  • Understand site traffic and performance

7. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Data may be processed by trusted third-party services that help us run the site, including hosting providers, analytics services, advertising services, and account/database providers.

8. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your data. However, no online service is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Your Rights

You can request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data. If you have a RobTechUK account, you may also be able to delete your account or request that we remove associated data such as profile information and leaderboard data.

10. Children

RobTechUK websites and games are not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children without appropriate permission. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us.

11. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

RobTechUK Music

RobTechUK Music is an independent music project created by the founder of RobTechUK, a lifelong music fan and award-winning composer.

All music used across RobTechUK games and projects is original, and varies from minimalist games music to piano music, ambient, dance and orchestral scores.

The project covers a range of styles including electronic, ambient, retro-inspired and experimental tracks, with new music being developed alongside ongoing game releases.

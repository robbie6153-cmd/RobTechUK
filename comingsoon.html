RobTechUK is continuing to grow from a collection of simple web games into a wider home for original games, football projects, social tools, and experimental ideas.

ScoreCast24 is now live

ScoreCast24 is ready to play now. The first version lets players predict World Cup scores, compete on a leaderboard, and test their football knowledge against friends, family, workmates, and other players.

A Premier League version is planned for the 2026/27 season, giving players the chance to predict weekly fixtures across the full campaign.

We are also hoping to trial a weekly draft fantasy football game, built around quick weekly selections rather than long, complicated season-long commitments.

Bigger projects for next year

RobTechUK is also aiming toward larger and more ambitious projects, including games based in real neighbourhoods and familiar local areas.

Want to see your own home area taken over by a zombie apocalypse? We’re working on it.

These ideas are still early-stage, but the long-term aim is to create games that feel personal, local, and different from the usual big-budget releases.

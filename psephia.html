Psephia

Psephia is a future social polling platform planned by RobTechUK. The idea is to create a space where users can vote on polls, track opinion trends, and take part in discussions across topics such as politics, sport, entertainment, food and drink, and more.

The project is intended to combine simple design with strong user interaction, making it easy to create, answer, and explore polls. Future plans will include user accounts, trending polls, category tabs, profiles, and community-driven discussions.

Psephia is currently in development and more details will be added soon.

RobTechUK – Independent UK Games Developer

RobTechUK is an independent UK games and web-projects brand creating free browser-based games, word puzzles, arcade games and interactive online platforms. The project began with simple word games and has grown into a wider collection of digital projects built for casual players, puzzle fans and people who enjoy quick online games.

The aim of RobTechUK is to create accessible, easy-to-play web games that work directly in the browser without downloads. Our projects include free word games, arcade-style games, score challenges, daily puzzles and new experimental ideas currently in development.

Free Online Word Games

RobTechUK started with a collection of free online word games designed for players who enjoy quick thinking, vocabulary challenges and daily puzzle-style gameplay. These games include word-building challenges, timed rounds, letter puzzles and leaderboard-based competition.

Visit the Word Games section to explore RobTechUK word games including Letters In, Word Shuffle, Word Steps and Word Scramble.

Arcade Games and Browser Projects

RobTechUK has also expanded into arcade-style browser games. These projects are designed to be simple, playable and fun on desktop or mobile devices. Current arcade projects include Cat Quest and Invaders, with more games planned for the future.

Visit the Arcade section to see RobTechUK arcade games, or try Cat Quest.

Psephia Polling Platform

Psephia is a RobTechUK web project focused on polls, opinions and public voting. It is being developed as a simple online polling platform where users can create polls, vote, compare opinions and explore different topics.

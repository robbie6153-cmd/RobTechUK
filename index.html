RobTechUK Music

RobTechUK Music is an independent music project focused on creating original soundtracks, experimental tracks and audio content for games, videos and online projects.

The music ranges from simple background tracks and game-style loops to more experimental ideas inspired by different genres including electronic, orchestral and retro-style sounds.

Music for Games and Projects

Many RobTechUK games and projects are designed with original audio in mind. The long-term goal is to create unique soundtracks for each game, giving every project its own identity.
